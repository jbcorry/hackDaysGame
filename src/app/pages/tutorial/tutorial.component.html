Time to kill while your code buids

Watching code build is exciting. Switch to another task and you may forget you had a build processing in the first place.

Play a quick game and get right back to your build as soon as you're done.

How to play

  • Choose to play for 2, 5, or 10 minutes.
  • Use arrow keys to move forward →, backward ←, and jump ↑ to avoid obstacles.
  • Score points based on how far you get and how many stars you collect within the given time.

Rules

  • You must sign up and log in to play.
  • You can play one round every 30 minutes. After all, the point is to effectively get back to what you were doing, right?
